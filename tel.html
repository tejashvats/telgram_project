#details

Telegram is a cloud-based instant messaging app that allows users to send and receive messages, photos, videos, and other files. It was created by Pavel Durov, the founder of Russia's largest social network, Vkontakte. Telegram was first launched in 2013 and has since grown to become one of the most popular messaging apps in the world. One of the key features of Telegram is its focus on privacy and security. The app uses end-to-end encryption to ensure that messages are kept private and secure, and it also offers a self-destruct timer for messages and the ability to create secret chats that are not stored on Telegram's servers. Telegram also supports group chats, channels, and bots. Group chats can accommodate up to 200,000 members and can be used for communication between friends, family, or coworkers. Channels, on the other hand, are used to broadcast messages to a large audience, and they can be public or private. Bots are automated programs that can be used to perform a wide range of tasks, such as scheduling reminders, getting news updates, or playing games. Telegram is available on multiple platforms, including iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux. The app is free to download and use, and it does not show any ads or collect any user data for advertising purposes.
